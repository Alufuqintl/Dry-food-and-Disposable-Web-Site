Our Story

Dedicated to elevating culinary experiences through superior ingredients.

A Passion for Quality

Al'ufuq International L.L.C is a dynamic and trusted trading company based in Dubai, UAE, specializing in the import, export, and distribution of high-quality food products and FMCG goods. With over 10 years of experience in international trade, we have built a strong reputation for reliability, transparency, and service excellence across global markets.

We serve a wide range of customers, including HORECA (Hotels, Restaurants, and Catering) clients, retailers, wholesalers, and distributors. Our portfolio covers canned goods, dry foods, beverages, frozen products, and essential commodities, all sourced and supplied in compliance with international quality and safety standards..

Al'ufuq International L.L.C exports to multiple countries across Africa, the Middle East, and Asia,supported by our deep understanding of global sourcing, efficient supply chain logistics, and evolving market needs. In addition to international exports, we are actively engaged in local wholesale trading within the UAE, ensuring timely supply and competitive pricing for our domestic partners.
